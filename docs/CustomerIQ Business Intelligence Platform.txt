CustomerIQ - Business Intelligence & Growth
Analytics Platform
Product Vision
CustomerIQ is a multi-tenant business intelligence platform that helps business owners upload sales
and customer datasets and instantly receive analytics, rankings, insights, reports, and growth
recommendations.
The goal is not just to display charts, but to explain what the data means and what actions the business
should take next.
